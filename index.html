Gem Guesser


This is the GEM GUESSER game! Each one of the four "gems" holds a certain random number (the first gem is lowest, the last gem is highest, but the numbers are random). You will have a target number to reach each time the game is played. Each time you click a gem, your own number will go up, until you either reach the target, or go over and lose! When you win or lose, a new number will appear for you to guess, and the gems will have different values!

Have fun! PRESS START TO BEGIN!
